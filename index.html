About Us

Southampton Rangers Sports Club (SRSC) is a community-rooted club in Southampton, Bermuda, with proud traditions in cricket and football. Our mission is to develop athletes, strengthen community ties, and provide a welcoming clubhouse for families and supporters.

Club History

From humble beginnings to a beloved community institution. Here are a few of our milestones.

  • 1959 — Club founded in Southampton.
  • 1960s–70s — First senior cricket and football teams established.
  • 1980s — Clubhouse improvements; youth programmes grow.
  • 1990s — League and cup successes across divisions.
  • 2000s — Community outreach expands; membership surges.
  • 2010s — Facilities upgrades and academy pathway.
  • Today — Competing proudly; investing in youth and community.
